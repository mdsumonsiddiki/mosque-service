Counseling Services

Every soul shall taste death, and you will indeed be paid your reward in full on the Day of Resurrection. And whosoever is distanced from the Fire and made to enter the Garden has certainly triumphed. And the life of this world is naught but the enjoyment of delusion.(Quran 3:185)

Our community today is faced with many challenges that are proving to be overwhelming and difficult to deal with; from faith-related issues, to social problems, to financial trouble, etc. The ICofCGleadership recognizes the seriousness of those problems that threaten the fabric of our families, affect our youth, and strike at the core of our community.

If you are facing any issues or conflicts in your personal life or with your family, our Imams at the ICofA are available to help. They will sit down with you and other parties (family, spouse, children, partners…etc) and offer guidance and advice to arrive to a favorable resolution. Please rest assured that all issues will be handled with sensitivity, compassion, and complete confidentiality.

Please know that you don’t have to deal with these trying times alone, and no matter how difficult the situation might be, Allah’s (swt) mercy and forgiveness are vast. Don’t hesitate to reach out to us by calling the Center at.
